Our Core Services

Lumen Estate stands as a distinguished leader in the Nigerian Real Estate Development and Construction sector, dedicated to shaping urban landscapes with innovation, integrity, and excellence. Our comprehensive range of services includes real estate development, civil engineering and construction works, facility management, and general contracts and merchandise, each contributing to our mission of building sustainable communities and delivering unparalleled value to our clients.

Real Estate Development

Our primary objective in real estate development is to create exceptional residential, commercial, and mixed-use properties that elevate living standards and contribute to the socio-economic growth of our communities. We conceptualize and execute projects that blend modern design, functionality, and environmental stewardship. From upscale residential estates to cutting-edge commercial complexes, our developments are tailored to meet the evolving needs and aspirations of our clients and the local market.

We are involved in:

  • Commercial Buildings,
  • Low cost/Mass housing,
  • Mixed-use Buildings,
  • Property Purchases & Partnership Opportunities,
  • Improved land parcel sales,
  • Investment acquisition,
  • Construction, and
  • build-to-suits for sale and lease purposes.

Civil Engineering & Construction Works

At Lumen-Deo, we excel in executing a diverse range of civil engineering and construction projects, encompassing infrastructure development, road construction, water supply systems, drainage solutions, and more. Facility Management

Beyond the completion of projects, Lumen-Deo offers comprehensive facility management services aimed at ensuring the optimal performance, functionality, and longevity of built environments. With a focus on proactive management and customer-centric service, we enable our clients to maximize their investments and achieve their operational goals with confidence.

General Contracts and Merchandise

In addition to our core services, Lumen-Deo offers general contracting services for various projects across different sectors.
